#  Introduction

Welcome to a comprehensive learning hub focused on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics—where artificial intelligence moves beyond screens and into the real world through embodied systems.

This documentation is built using Docusaurus, providing a fast, structured, and scalable way to learn, document, and experiment with topics such as perception, motion, control, cognition, and human–robot interaction.

This textbook covers:

- ROS 2 (Robotic Nervous System)

- Simulation with Gazebo & Unity (Digital Twins)

- NVIDIA Isaac Sim & Isaac ROS

- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems
